Privacy Policy

Fitness Kingdom

Effective Date: 28/07/2025

Fitness Kingdom values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Fitness Kingdom does not directly collect, store, or sell personal data through the app.

However, limited information may be involved in the following cases:

  • Optional Feedback Submission
    If you voluntarily submit feedback or suggestions through an external Google Form, you may choose to provide your name and email address. This information is collected only if you choose to submit it and is not required to use the app.
  • Crash & Performance Data
    Anonymous diagnostic and performance data may be collected automatically by platform providers such as Apple App Store or Google Play Services to help identify crashes and improve app stability. This data is not linked to your identity.

How Information Is Used

  • To improve app performance and stability
  • To review user feedback when voluntarily provided

Fitness Kingdom does not use data for advertising, marketing, tracking, or profiling.

Third-Party Services

Fitness Kingdom may rely on third-party services that operate under their own privacy policies, including:

  • Apple App Store Services
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Forms (for optional feedback collection)

These services may collect information in accordance with their respective privacy policies. Fitness Kingdom does not control or store this data.

Data Security

Fitness Kingdom does not maintain its own servers for storing personal data. We are committed to protecting user privacy and minimizing data exposure.

Children’s Privacy

Fitness Kingdom does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 and is not directed toward children.
